# AC-20260703-postpoc-004 Baseline Promotion Workflow Evidence

## Scope

Acceptance for `TASK-POSTPOC-004` /
`POSTPOC-PKT-004-baseline-promotion`.

Implemented a local baseline promotion evaluator and review-packet builder
that reduces propose, approve, freeze, expire, revoke, supersede, and reject
events into baseline state, promotion request records, decision records,
immutability events, reviewer checklist items, candidate-vs-previous
comparison deltas, and hold findings. The CLI approval surface is present; the
browser approval UI remains separate productization work.

## Requirements

- `HATE-POSTPOC-GAP-004`
- `POSTPOC-PKT-004-baseline-promotion`
- `POST_POC_PRODUCTIZATION_DETAIL_SPEC.md#hate-postpoc-gap-004-baseline-promotion-workflow`
- `WORKFLOW_COOKBOOK_OPERATION_CONTRACT.md`

## Acceptance Criteria

- Propose, approve, and freeze produce replayable baseline state transitions.
- Promotion requests and decisions preserve actor, reviewer, reason, policy
  hash, expiry, and evidence references.
- Baseline mutation produces immutable before/after sourceRefs.
- Actor/reviewer self approval is denied.
- Expired and revoked baselines cannot be used for comparison.
- Proposed-only or otherwise unapproved baselines cannot hide regressions.
- Missing candidate evidence and missing reviewer produce hold findings.
- Baseline review packets expose required reviewer checklist items.
- Candidate-vs-previous score and regression deltas are explicit.
- Review packets cannot become ready while promotion findings or new
  regressions remain.
- Review packets can be written as deterministic local artifacts.
