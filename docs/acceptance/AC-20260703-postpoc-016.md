# AC-20260703-postpoc-016 Human Review Workflow Evidence

## Scope

Acceptance for `TASK-POSTPOC-016` /
`POSTPOC-PKT-016-human-review-workflow`.

Implemented a deterministic human review workflow evaluator and queue-packet
builder that models reviewer assignment, evidence attachment, approve, deny,
revoke, expire, supersede, resolve, and replay actions. It produces request,
decision, evidence attachment, queue item, allowed action, and SLA handoff
records without turning human review into a waiver bypass.

## Acceptance Criteria

- A review can be assigned, evidence can be attached, and approval can replay
  deterministically.
- Denial with evidence and reason is recorded as a first-class decision.
- Approval without evidence produces a hold finding.
- Missing reviewer produces a hold finding.
- Owner self approval is denied.
- Revoked and expired decisions cannot release a hold.
- Replay mismatch produces a hold finding.
- Supersede and resolve actions are represented without bypassing evidence.
- Human review queue packets expose reviewer, owner, due date, expiry date,
  required decision, evidence refs, and allowed actions.
- Queue packets require review identity, reviewer, due date, and expiry date.
- Queue packets cannot be ready while workflow findings remain.
- Queue packets can be written as deterministic local artifacts.
