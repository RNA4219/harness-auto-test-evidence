# AC-20260703-postpoc-012 Store Backup/Restore DR Evidence

## Scope

Acceptance for `TASK-POSTPOC-012` /
`POSTPOC-PKT-012-store-dr`.

Implemented a deterministic store DR evaluator and restore runbook builder
that emits backup, restore, DR drill, and DR runbook records. It verifies backup
inventory, hash consistency, corruption scan status, legal hold preservation,
RTO/RPO budgets, projection rebuild completion, and required restore steps
without requiring a managed cloud backup service.

## Requirements

- `HATE-POSTPOC-GAP-012`
- `POSTPOC-PKT-012-store-dr`
- `POST_POC_PRODUCTIZATION_DETAIL_SPEC.md#hate-postpoc-gap-012-store-backup-restore-and-dr-operations`
- `WORKFLOW_COOKBOOK_OPERATION_CONTRACT.md`

## Acceptance Criteria

- Successful restore drills produce backup, restore, and DR drill evidence.
- Legal hold counts are preserved across restore.
- Corrupt or unscanned backups are denied before becoming canonical evidence.
- Missing backup inventory or manifest references hold the DR report.
- Backup hash mismatch holds the DR report.
- RTO budget exceedance is explicit.
- Projection rebuild failure is explicit.
- DR runbooks expose required restore verification steps.
- Corrupt backups, RTO exceedance, legal hold loss, and projection rebuild
  failure block restore readiness.
- DR runbooks can be written as deterministic local artifacts.
