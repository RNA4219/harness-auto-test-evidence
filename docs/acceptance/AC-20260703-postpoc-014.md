# AC-20260703-postpoc-014 Compliance and Procurement Evidence

## Scope

Acceptance for `TASK-POSTPOC-014` /
`POSTPOC-PKT-014-compliance-procurement`.

Implemented a deterministic compliance evidence evaluator that models control
claims, reviewer decisions, compliance evidence packs, and customer-safe
procurement exports. It verifies evidence references, reviewer approval, claim
freshness, supported procurement answers, and redaction safety.

## Requirements

- `HATE-POSTPOC-GAP-014`
- `POSTPOC-PKT-014-compliance-procurement`
- `POST_POC_PRODUCTIZATION_DETAIL_SPEC.md#hate-postpoc-gap-014-compliance-and-procurement-evidence`
- `WORKFLOW_COOKBOOK_OPERATION_CONTRACT.md`

## Acceptance Criteria

- Procurement export is safe to share and links every claim to evidence.
- Control claims require supported claim class, claim text, and evidence refs.
- Stale control claims or stale review decisions hold the report.
- Missing reviewer approval holds the report.
- Customer-safe exports with restricted data or missing redaction reports hold.
- Unsupported procurement answers hold.
